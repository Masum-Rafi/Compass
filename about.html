About Compass

Compass is your online travel companion, bringing you detailed guides, inspiring travel stories, and essential resources to make your journeys unforgettable. Whether you’re exploring the hidden gems of Bangladesh or adventuring abroad, Compass is here to guide your path.

Founded in 2025, Compass aims to connect travelers with authentic experiences, reliable information, and a vibrant community of explorers.

Our Mission

  • Provide reliable travel guides and tips
  • Promote cultural understanding and local heritage
  • Encourage responsible and sustainable tourism
